ORIGIN OF THE VILLAIN

Born Daniel Dumile, MF DOOM first emerged as Zev Love X of KMD. After the passing of his brother Subroc, he vanished from the industry, returning years later masked as a comic-book supervillain.

TIMELINE

Late 1980s

Creates KMD and records as Zev Love X, stepping into the underground hip‑hop scene.

Early 1990s

KMD gains recognition, but tragedy and label issues push Dumile into a long hiatus.

Late 1990s

Reappears at open mics with his face obscured, slowly building the MF DOOM persona.

1999

Releases Operation: Doomsday, fully debuting the masked supervillain identity.

2003–2004

Drops key projects like Vaudeville Villain, Take Me to Your Leader, and Madvillainy, solidifying his legend.

2004–2009

Continues to evolve with releases like Mm..Food and Born Like This, plus numerous collabs.

2010s & Beyond

Focuses on collaborations and rare appearances, while his influence spreads across generations of artists.
